## 生成图片的 Prompt 参考

### 标准状态 (Clean)
```
A clean and organized railway signal equipment training laboratory in a Chinese vocational college. The room has multiple workbenches with signal equipment including colored light signal models, electric switch machines, and track circuit training stations. All tools are neatly arranged on tool boards. Safety helmets and protective gloves are properly stored on hooks. The floor is spotlessly clean with clear walking paths marked with yellow lines. Fluorescent lighting illuminates the space evenly. Professional technical posters about railway signal systems are displayed on walls. Photorealistic style, wide angle view.
```

### 异常状态 (Messy)
```
A messy and disorganized railway signal equipment training laboratory in a Chinese vocational college. The room has workbenches with signal equipment but tools are scattered randomly on tables and floor. Some safety helmets are left on chairs instead of hooks. Cables are tangled and not properly coiled. There is trash on the floor including paper and plastic bottles. A broom and dustpan are left in the middle of the walkway. Some equipment drawers are left open. The overall impression is cluttered and unsafe. Photorealistic style, wide angle view.
```
